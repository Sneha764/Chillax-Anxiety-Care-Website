Yoga Exercises for Relaxation

Child's Pose

Instructions: Kneel on the floor, sit on your heels, and bend forward, resting your forehead on the ground. Stretch your arms in front of you or alongside your body.

Cat-Cow Stretch

Instructions: Start on your hands and knees. Inhale as you arch your back (Cow), and exhale as you round your back (Cat).

Seated Forward Bend

Instructions: Sit with your legs extended in front of you. Inhale and lengthen your spine, then exhale as you bend forward, reaching for your feet.
